How the Finger Chooser Works

  • Everyone ready? Pass the smartphone or tablet around the group. Each participating person places one finger simultaneously on the dark area.
  • Hold still! As soon as at least 2 fingers are detected, the countdown starts automatically. All fingers must remain on the screen for 2 seconds.
  • The chosen one lights up! The chooser randomly selects one finger – that spot lights up and grows larger. That person is selected!
  • Again? Tap "Reset" and start a new round.

When is the Finger Chooser ideal?

Who starts the card game? Who asks the question today? Who pays for the first coffee? The Finger Chooser settles such group decisions in seconds – fairly, fun, and without debate. Everyone is involved simultaneously, no one can dodge, and the result is visible to all.

The tool is especially popular at party game nights, spontaneous decision rounds with friends, in the classroom for fairly selecting students, as well as in team games and group activities. Find more ideas on the party games page.
