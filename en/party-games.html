🎭 Truth or Dare – The Perfect Template

Few party games are as timeless and versatile as Truth or Dare. With SpinSelector it becomes even fairer and more exciting: enter all participant names into the wheel and let fate decide who has to answer a question or complete a dare next. No more arguing about whose turn it is!

Version 1: Choosing a person with the wheel

Enter all player names into the wheel. Each round, the previously selected person spins the wheel – whoever it lands on must choose between Truth or Dare. Only enable "Auto-remove" if you want to make sure each person goes exactly once. For classic Truth or Dare, it's usually better left disabled.

Version 2: Challenge wheel

Create a second wheel with the challenges themselves: enter specific truth questions and dare tasks into the wheel. The currently selected person then spins this challenge wheel. This way the challenges are double-randomised – both who goes and what they have to do.

🗣️ Truth or Dare – Classic

Ideal for groups of 4+, ages 16 and up. Spin the wheel to select a person, then they decide themselves.

👥 Splitting Groups & Teams Fairly

Whether at a sports event, school trip, company outing or game night: splitting teams fairly and neutrally is often a challenge. With SpinSelector, dividing groups becomes a fair spectacle:

How to split teams with the spin wheel

Enter all participant names. Enable "Auto-remove". Spin the wheel once for Team A – the winner goes into Team A and is removed. Spin again for Team B, and so on. Repeat until everyone has been assigned. Quick, fair and visible to everyone!

For even more fun: enter the team names into the wheel (instead of people) and let each person spin for themselves to find out which team they're on.

Pro Tip: With an odd number of participants, use the wheel to decide who forms a team alone or who takes on the referee role. This avoids awkward situations and ensures full transparency.

💡 Tips for the Perfect Party Setup

To get the most out of SpinSelector at your next party, here are the most important tips:

  • Prepare in advance: Set up your wheel configuration before the party starts and leave the browser open – your options are saved automatically.
  • Big screen: Connect your smartphone or laptop to a TV via HDMI or casting so everyone can see the wheel. This hugely increases the suspense.
  • Prepare multiple wheels: Open SpinSelector in several browser tabs with different configurations – e.g. one tab with names, one with challenges.
  • Shuffle before each round: Click "Shuffle" before a new round begins. This makes patterns on the wheel impossible and keeps the tension up.
  • Dark mode: In a dimly lit party room the wheel looks even more impressive in dark mode. Switch it on via the moon icon in the top right corner.
