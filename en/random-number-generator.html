Random Number Generator – Fair, Fast, Cryptographically Secure

The Random Number Generator on SpinSelector produces true random numbers using window.crypto.getRandomValues() – the cryptographically secure random function of modern browsers. The result is mathematically unpredictable and non-reproducible: no pseudo-randomness, no hidden patterns.

All calculations run exclusively in your browser – no data ever leaves your device. The generator is free, requires no login, and works on any modern smartphone, tablet or desktop.

What is the Generator good for?

Roll dice without dice: Simply choose the 1–6 quick range and generate – instantly you have a fair dice roll. For board games, role-playing games or spontaneous decisions. Lottery numbers: With the quickpick button you get 6 unique, random numbers in the range 1 to 49, just like a real lottery ticket. Assign tasks: Give participants numbers and let the tool select the next task. For teachers: Random page numbers, task numbers or group order in seconds – more ideas on the classroom page.
